830.5: Issuing No Objection Certificate for Valid Flight

Description

This service enables the customers to obtain Initial online approval for the carriage of Firearms in accordance with Civil Aviation Regulations of the UAE. This service is subject to final approval from Airport Police. This service is mandatory and all requests should be submitted by Airlines only.

Location
Online
Applicable For
Airlines
Required Documents

1. Valid Passport copy.
2. Valid Flight License ( if required by the country ).
3. Flight details (type, location, id number).
4. Official letter stating the reason for use of flight:
- Hunting: from Travel agencies.
- Sport competition: from organizer.
- VIP protection: from Government entities.
